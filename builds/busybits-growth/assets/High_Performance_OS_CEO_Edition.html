☀️ Module 1: The Morning Protocol (07:00 - 09:00)

Goal: Cortisol Peak + Dopamine Baseline.

1. The "Hydration Bomb" (07:05)

  • Protocol: 500ml Water + 1/4 tsp Himalayan Salt + 1/2 Lemon.
  • Why: Rehydrates the brain after 8h of sleep. Salt supports adrenal function.

2. Forward Ambulation (07:15)

  • Protocol: 10-20 minute walk outside (No sunglasses).
  • Why: Optic flow suppresses amygdala activity (anxiety). Sunlight sets the circadian clock for better sleep tonight.

3. The "90-Minute Block" (07:45 - 09:15)

  • Rule: Phone in another room (Airplane Mode). No Email. No Slack.
  • Task: The #1 most important task of the day (Eat The Frog).
  • Why: Your brain's decision fatigue is lowest. Do the hard thing first.

NSDR (Non-Sleep Deep Rest)

🌙 Module 4: The Evening Wind Down (20:00 - 22:00)

Goal: Reduce core body temperature + Melatonin onset.

The "3-2-1" Rule

  • 3 Hours before bed: Stop eating (Digestion ruins Deep Sleep).
  • 2 Hours before bed: Stop working (Cortisol drop).

Thermal Dump

  • Protocol: Hot shower/sauna for 10-15 mins.
  • Why: Rapid cooling afterwards signals the body to sleep.
