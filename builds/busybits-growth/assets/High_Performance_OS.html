👋 Start Here: The Philosophy

Most people run their lives on Default Mode (Reactive: Email, Slack, News).

Top performers run on Designed Mode (Proactive: Deep Work, Recovery, Systems).

This Operating System is not about working harder. It is about resource allocation. We treat your Time, Energy, and Focus as finite capital to be invested, not spent.

Module 1

The Morning Protocol

07:05

The "Hydration Bomb"

Protocol: 500ml Water + 1/4 tsp Himalayan Salt + 1/2 Lemon.

Why: Rehydrates the brain after 8h of sleep. Salt supports adrenal function.

07:15

Forward Ambulation

Protocol: 10-20 minute walk outside (No sunglasses).

Why: Optic flow suppresses amygdala activity (anxiety). Sunlight sets the circadian clock for better sleep tonight.

07:45

The "90-Minute Block"

Rule: Phone in another room (Airplane Mode). No Email. No Slack.

Task: The #1 most important task of the day (Eat The Frog).

Why: Your brain's decision fatigue is lowest. Do the hard thing first.

Module 2

The "Deep Work" Algorithm

The 90/20 Rule

Based on Ultradian Rhythms.

  • ● Focus: 90 Minutes intense work.
  • ● Rest: 20 Minutes detached rest.
  • ● Limit: Max 3 cycles per day.

NSDR (Non-Sleep Deep Rest)

Use when you hit a wall at 2pm. Replaces caffeine.

  • Protocol: 10-20 minute guided relaxation (Yoga Nidra).
  • Effect: Restores dopamine reserves by 40%.
