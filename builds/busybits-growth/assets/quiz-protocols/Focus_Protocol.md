# The Monkey Mind Protocol (Type C)

**Your Bottleneck:** Focus.
**Your Symptom:** Starting 10 things, finishing 0. Distracted by notifications.
**Your Fix:** Environment Design, Dopamine Detox, Deep Work.

---

## 1. The Environment Design Rule
Willpower is a finite resource. If you rely on willpower to ignore your phone, you will fail by 3 PM.
You need to design your environment so focus is the default.

**The Fix:**
-   **Phone:** Charge it in another room.
-   **Slack/Email:** Turn off notifications completely. Check batch 2x/day.
-   **Browser:** Close all tabs except the one you are working on.

## 2. The Dopamine Detox
Your brain is addicted to "cheap dopamine" (Likes, retweets, endless scroll).
This makes "boring" work (like writing or coding) feel painful.

**The Rule:** No social media for the first 4 hours of the day.
Make your brain "bored" enough to crave work.

## 3. The Tech Stack for Focus
-   **Opal:** Block apps (Twitter, Instagram, TikTok) from 9-5.
-   **Superhuman:** Email is a focus killer. Batch process it.
-   **Notion:** One place for all ideas. If it's not written down, it's a distraction.
