Medical Disclaimer

This is not medical advice. This is a survival protocol. Do not do this every day. Chronic sleep deprivation kills performance (and you). Use this only when shipping, launching, or putting out fires.

1

Morning The Adenosine Flush

0-5 Mins: The Cold Shock

Do not look at your phone. Get out of bed immediately. Splash ice-cold water on your face or take a 60-second cold shower. This spikes adrenaline/cortisol to wake you up.

5-15 Mins: The Light Blast

Get sunlight in your eyes immediately (10-15 mins). No sun? Use a 10,000 lux sad lamp. Sets your circadian clock so you can sleep tonight.

15-30 Mins: Hydrate First

Drink 500ml water with electrolytes. WAIT 90 MINUTES BEFORE COFFEE.

The Science: Drinking coffee immediately blocks adenosine receptors but doesn't clear the chemical. When caffeine wears off at 2pm, the crash is brutal. Let your body clear it naturally first.
2

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM The "Napuccino"

  1. Drink a double espresso rapidly.
  2. Immediately set a timer for 20 minutes.
  3. Lie down and close your eyes (even if you don't sleep).
  4. Wake up exactly when the alarm rings.

The nap clears adenosine. Caffeine takes 20 mins to hit the bloodstream. You wake up with a cleared brain AND a caffeine kick.
