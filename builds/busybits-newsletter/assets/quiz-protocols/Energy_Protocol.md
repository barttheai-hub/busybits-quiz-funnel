# The Energy Crasher Protocol (Type B)

**Your Bottleneck:** Energy.
**Your Symptom:** High peaks, deep valleys. 3 PM burnout. Waking up groggy.
**Your Fix:** Circadian Rhythm, Nutrition, Recovery.

---

## 1. The Glucose Rollercoaster
Most entrepreneurs crash at 3 PM because they spike their insulin at 1 PM.
If you eat a heavy carb lunch, your body shuttles blood to digestion. Your brain shuts down.

**The Fix:**
-   **Breakfast:** High Protein + Fats (Eggs, Avocado, Steak). No simple carbs.
-   **Lunch:** Salad + Protein. No bread, pasta, or sugar.
-   **Dinner:** Carbs are allowed (help with sleep).

## 2. The Caffeine Half-Life
Caffeine has a 6-hour half-life.
If you drink coffee at 4 PM, 50% of it is still in your brain at 10 PM.
This destroys **Deep Sleep** (recovery).

**The Rule:** No caffeine after 12:00 PM.
Switch to Decaf or electrolytes.

## 3. The Tech Stack for Energy
-   **Oura Ring / Whoop:** Track HRV. If HRV drops, reduce workout intensity.
-   **Eight Sleep:** Temperature regulation is the #1 lever for sleep quality.
-   **BusyBody App:** Track your macros to ensure stable energy.
