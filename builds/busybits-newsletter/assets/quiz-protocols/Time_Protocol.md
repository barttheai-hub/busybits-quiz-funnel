# The Time-Starved Founder Protocol (Type A)

**Your Bottleneck:** Time.
**Your Symptom:** High output, low leverage. You feel like you're drowning in operations.
**Your Fix:** Delete, Delegate, Automate.

---

## 1. The "Eisenhower Matrix" on Steroids
Most people categorize tasks by "Urgent vs Important."
You need to categorize by **Leverage**.

**The Framework:**
-   **$10/hr Work:** Admin, Scheduling, Inbox Zero. -> **DELEGATE.**
-   **$100/hr Work:** Project Management, Editing, Sales Calls. -> **SYSTEMIZE.**
-   **$1,000/hr Work:** Strategy, Hiring, Deal Making. -> **DO.**
-   **$10,000/hr Work:** Vision, Public Speaking, Content Creation. -> **DOUBLE DOWN.**

**Action:** Audit your last 48 hours. Circle every task below your hourly aspirational rate. Stop doing them.

## 2. The "No-Meeting" Morning
You are bleeding focus by switching contexts.
Meetings are for managers. Deep work is for makers.

**The Rule:** No meetings before 12:00 PM.
Use the morning for your $1,000/hr work.

## 3. The Tech Stack for Time
-   **Superhuman:** Cut email time by 50%.
-   **Zapier:** Automate repetitive data entry.
-   **Loom:** Stop typing explanations. Record a 2-min video instead.
