Meal Rule Examples

Lunch Protein + Fiber Chicken Salad, Salmon. NO PASTA.
Dinner Carbs Allowed Rice, Potatoes (Helps with sleep/serotonin).

2. The Caffeine Half-Life

Caffeine has a 6-hour half-life. If you drink coffee at 4 PM, 50% of it is still active at 10 PM. This destroys Deep Sleep.

The Rule

No caffeine after 12:00 PM.
Switch to Decaf, sparkling water, or electrolytes in the afternoon.
