# Tactical Notes from @nilisty (X) — Adapted for Mila IG

Source observed directly on profile timeline (recent + pinned): https://x.com/nilisty

## Patterns that seem to work
1. Strong first-line hooks (opinionated, immediate context)
2. Behind-the-scenes prompt/process reveals increase saves/bookmarks
3. Clear contrarian framing ("most people focus on X, real lever is Y")
4. Timing discipline (explicit mention of 8–10am EST windows)
5. Replies used as distribution engine, not just likes

## How we adapt this to Instagram
- Start captions with sharp, polarizing-but-safe first line
- Show process (routine structure, not just glam output)
- Use save-worthy list formats in carousels
- Use story polls/questions to farm intent signals
- Use pinned comments for action loops (comment keyword CTA)

## Prompts/Tactic templates for Mila
- Hook template: "Most people fail because they chase [extreme]. Real progress is [simple repeatable behavior]."
- Carousel template: "5 rules I follow when motivation is low"
- Story template: check-in -> friction -> action -> poll -> CTA

## Guardrail
We are copying mechanics (hooking, structure, CTA loops), NOT identity or content style directly.
