# The BusyBits Sleep Optimization Checklist (Recovery Protocol)

**Objective:** Maximize Deep & REM sleep for cognitive performance.
**Metric:** Sleep Score > 85 (Oura/Whoop/Eight Sleep).

---

## The "3-2-1" Rule (Pre-Bed Routine)
*   [ ] **3 Hours Before Bed:** Stop eating.
    *   Digestion requires energy and raises core body temp.
    *   *Exception:* Small protein/fat snack if hungry (e.g., spoonful of almond butter).
*   [ ] **2 Hours Before Bed:** Stop working.
    *   Cortisol needs to drop. Close the laptop.
    *   No email, no Slack, no "just one quick thing."
*   [ ] **1 Hour Before Bed:** No screens.
    *   Blue light suppresses melatonin.
    *   *Alternative:* Read fiction (paper book) or listen to a podcast/audiobook.

## The Environment (The Cave)
*   [ ] **Temperature:** Set thermostat to 65-68°F (18-20°C).
    *   Body temp must drop to initiate sleep.
*   [ ] **Light:** Pitch black.
    *   Blackout curtains or a high-quality sleep mask (Manta/Oura).
    *   Cover all LEDs (router, charger, TV standby light) with electrical tape.
*   [ ] **Sound:** Silence or White Noise.
    *   Earplugs (Loop Quiet) if in a noisy city.
    *   Fan or White Noise machine if needed.

## The Supplements (30 Mins Before)
*   [ ] **Magnesium Glycinate/Threonate:** 400mg.
*   [ ] **L-Theanine:** 200mg (optional, for racing thoughts).
*   [ ] **Apigenin:** 50mg (optional, sedative).
*   *Avoid:* Melatonin (habit-forming, messes with natural production).

## The Morning Reset (Circadian Anchor)
*   [ ] **Sunlight:** Get outside within 30 mins of waking.
    *   10-20 mins of direct sunlight (no sunglasses/windows).
    *   Sets the circadian clock for tonight's sleep.
*   [ ] **Delay Caffeine:** Wait 90 mins before first coffee.
    *   Let adenosine clear naturally to avoid the afternoon crash.

---

## Troubleshooting
*   **Waking up at 3AM?** Could be blood sugar crash (eat a small snack before bed) or alcohol (stop drinking earlier).
*   **Can't fall asleep?** "4-7-8" Breathing: Inhale 4s, Hold 7s, Exhale 8s. Repeat 4x.
*   **Racing mind?** Write down tomorrow's "Big 3" tasks (clear the open loops).
