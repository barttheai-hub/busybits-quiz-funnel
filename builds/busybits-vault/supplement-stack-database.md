## The "Do Not Buy" List (Overrated/Useless)
*   **Generic Multivitamins:** Low bioavailability, wrong forms.
*   **BCAA:** Just eat protein.
*   **Fat Burners:** Unless it's caffeine/yohimbine, likely useless.
*   **Testosterone Boosters:** Tribulus/Fenugreek do almost nothing for actual test levels. Sleep more instead.

## Protocol Examples

### The "Deep Work" Morning
*   Hydrate: 16oz Water + Electrolytes
*   Fuel: Black Coffee + L-Theanine (200mg) + Alpha-GPC (300mg)
*   Result: 4 hours of locked-in focus.

### The "Recovery" Evening
*   Dinner: High Protein + Carbs
*   Wind Down: Magnesium Glycinate (400mg) + Glycine (3g)
*   Result: Deep, restorative sleep.
