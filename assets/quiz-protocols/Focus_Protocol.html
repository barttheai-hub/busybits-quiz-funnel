The Monkey Mind

**Your Bottleneck:** Focus.
**Your Fix:** Environment Design, Dopamine Detox, Deep Work.

1. The Environment Design Rule

Willpower is a finite resource. If you rely on willpower to ignore your phone, you will fail by 3 PM. Design your environment so focus is the default.

  • 📱
    Phone Charge it in another room (Kitchen/Hallway).
  • 💬
    Slack / Email Turn off notifications. Batch check 2x per day (11am & 4pm).
  • 🖥️
    Browser Close all tabs except the one you are working on.

2. The Dopamine Detox

Your brain is addicted to "cheap dopamine" (Likes, retweets, endless scroll). This makes "boring" work (like writing) feel painful.

The Rule

No social media for the first 4 hours of the day.
Make your brain "bored" enough to actually crave doing difficult work.
