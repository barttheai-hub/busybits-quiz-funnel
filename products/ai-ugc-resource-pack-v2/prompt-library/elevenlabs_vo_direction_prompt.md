# ElevenLabs Voiceover Direction

## Recommended Settings
- **Model:** Eleven Multilingual v2 (Better emotion than Turbo).
- **Stability:** 35% (Allows for more natural "imperfect" delivery).
- **Similarity:** 80% (Keeps the voice identity clear).
- **Style Exaggeration:** 10% (Just enough to not sound flat).

## Prompting the "Performance"
*Add these instructions BEFORE the text in your prompt to steer the AI.*

### The "Secret" Tone (For "I Fired Them" Hooks)
`[Instruction: Whisper slightly, confidential tone, fast pace]`
"I probably shouldn't be telling you this..."

### The "Angry/Frustrated" Tone (For Pain Points)
`[Instruction: Annoyed, sighing, slower pace]`
"I tried everything. The foams. The pills. It was a nightmare."

### The "Excited" Tone (For The Pivot)
`[Instruction: Upbeat, faster pace, smiling voice]`
"And then I found this. It completely changed the game."

### The "Authoritative" Tone (For Scientific Proof)
`[Instruction: Deep, calm, slow, confident]`
"The data doesn't lie. 80% of men see results in 90 days."
