# The 20 "Money" Hooks for Meta Ads

1.  **The "Stop Doing This" Hook**
    "Stop [Common Action]. It's destroying your [Asset]."
    *Example: "Stop applying Minoxidil foam. It's destroying your scalp pH."*

2.  **The "I Fired Them" Hook**
    "I just fired my [Service Provider]. Here is why."
    *Example: "I just fired my barber. Here is why."*

3.  **The "3 Mistakes" Hook**
    "If you have [Problem], you are probably making these 3 mistakes."
    *Example: "If you have thinning hair, you are probably making these 3 mistakes."*

4.  **The "Green Screen" Roast**
    "See this? This is [Popular Product]. Here is why it's a scam."
    *Example: "See this red light helmet? It's $800 of plastic."*

5.  **The "Algorithm" Hook**
    "The algorithm doesn't want you to know this..."
    *Example: "Big Pharma doesn't want you to know this about DHT."*

6.  **The "Founder Story" (Vulnerable)**
    "I built this because I was tired of [Pain]."
    *Example: "I built this because I was tired of hiding under a hat."*

7.  **The "Cost Comparison" (The Receipt)**
    "Here is what I spent vs what I got."
    *Example: "Transplant: $12,000. This Bottle: $40."*

8.  **The "ASMR" Unboxing**
    (No talking for 3 seconds, just satisfying sounds).

9.  **The "X vs Y" Split Screen**
    "My Morning Routine (Old) vs My Morning Routine (New)."

10. **The "Secret Weapon"**
    "This is my secret weapon for [Result]."

11. **The "Reason Why"**
    "The real reason your [X] isn't working."

12. **The "If You Like X, You'll Love Y"**
    "If you like [Competitor], you'll love [My Product] because..."

13. **The "Sketch" (Skit)**
    "Me trying to [Action] before I found [Product]."

14. **The "Data" Hook**
    "80% of men get this wrong."

15. **The "Transformation" (Reverse)**
    "Watch my [Result] disappear in 30 days... (Wait for it)."

16. **The "Question"**

17. **The "Tutorial"**
    "How to fix [Problem] in 3 steps."

18. **The "Hack"**
    "This [Industry] hack saved me [Amount]."

19. **The "Don't Buy"**
    "Don't buy [Product] until you watch this."

20. **The "Guarantee"**
    "If this doesn't fix your [Problem] in 30 days, I'll pay you."
