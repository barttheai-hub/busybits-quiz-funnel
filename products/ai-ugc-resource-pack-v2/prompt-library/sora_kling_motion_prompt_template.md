# Motion Prompts (Sora / Kling / Runway)

*Use these suffixes to add life to your static Midjourney images.*

## Camera Movements
- **Pan:** `slow pan right, cinematic motion`
- **Zoom:** `slow zoom in, intense focus`
- **Push:** `dolly push forward, depth parallax`
- **Handheld:** `handheld camera shake, authentic ugc vibe`

## Action Triggers (The "Verb" List)
- **Applying:** `applying cream to face, rubbing motion`
- **Drinking:** `sipping coffee, swallowing, natural movement`
- **Typing:** `fingers typing on keyboard, screen glow reflection`
- **Walking:** `walking towards camera, clothes moving in wind`
- **Talking:** `lips moving, talking to camera, facial expressions` (Note: Lip sync usually requires a separate tool like HeyGen/SyncLabs, but this adds general life).

## The "Glitch" Fixer
If the face distorts:
`--motion 3` (Lower motion value = less distortion).
