# Hair Loss Funnel - CRO Experiment Plan (Launch Month)

**Objective:** Systematic testing to improve Conversion Rate (CVR) from 1.5% -> 3.0%+.
**Traffic Source:** Meta/TikTok Ads.
**Metric to Optimize:** Revenue Per Visitor (RPV).

---

## Phase 1: The "Click" (Advertorial Bridge) - Days 1-7
*Goal: Get cheap clicks from Ad -> Advertorial -> VSL Page.*

### Experiment 1: The Headline (The "Hook")
-   **Control:** "Leading Dermatologists Call It 'The Great Awakening': Why 80% of Thinning Men Still Have Live Follicles."
-   **Variant A (Fear):** "The 'Pinch Test': If Your Scalp Is Tight, Your Hair Is Dying (Here's How To Save It)."
-   **Variant B (Curiosity):** "Why Men in Japan Aren't Going Bald (It's Not Diet)."
-   **Metric:** CTR to VSL Page.

### Experiment 2: The "Hero" Image
-   **Control:** Scientific diagram of "Constricted Blood Vessel".
-   **Variant A:** "Before/After" split face (Compliant/Safe).
-   **Variant B:** Man checking his hairline in mirror (Relatable/Emotional).
-   **Metric:** Time on Page + CTR.

---

## Phase 2: The "Watch" (VSL Page) - Days 8-14
*Goal: Increase VSL Watch Rate (Retention).*

### Experiment 3: The Video Player
-   **Control:** Standard Embed (Wistia/Vimeo).
-   **Variant A:** "Sticky" Player (Floats in corner as user scrolls).
-   **Variant B:** Autoplay (Muted) with captions vs Click-to-Play.
-   **Metric:** Average Watch Time / % Completed to Pitch (8:00 mark).

### Experiment 4: The "Buy Button" Timing
-   **Control:** Button appears immediately (Below video).
-   **Variant A:** Delayed Button (Appears only when Pitch starts in video).
-   **Metric:** Add to Cart (ATC) Rate.

---

## Phase 3: The "Sale" (Checkout & Offer) - Days 15-30
*Goal: Increase Average Order Value (AOV).*

### Experiment 5: The Bundle Highlight
-   **Control:** 3-Month Kit highlighted as "Most Popular".
-   **Variant A:** 6-Month Kit highlighted as "Best Value" (with Free Scalp Massager).
-   **Metric:** AOV.

### Experiment 6: The Guarantee Positioning
-   **Control:** Guarantee mentioned in footer.
-   **Variant A:** Guarantee Badge *inside* the Buy Button ("Risk-Free").
-   **Variant B:** "Double Your Money Back" (Aggressive - risky but high conversion).
-   **Metric:** Conversion Rate (CVR).

---

## Technical Stack for Testing
-   **Landing Page:** Unbounce / Replo (Shopify).
-   **Split Testing:** Intelligems (Shopify) or VWO.
-   **Heatmaps:** Microsoft Clarity (Free) to see where users drop off.

## Success Criteria (Benchmarks)
-   **Ad CTR:** > 1.0%
-   **Advertorial CTR:** > 20% (Click through to VSL)
-   **VSL Watch Rate:** > 25% reach the Pitch.
-   **Sales Page CVR:** > 2.0% (Cold Traffic).
