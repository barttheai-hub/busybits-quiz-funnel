# Hair Loss Funnel - Offer & Monetization Strategy (AOV Boost)

**Objective:** Increase Average Order Value (AOV) from ~$80 to >$110 to liquidate higher ad costs.
**Current Problem:** Standard E-com offer (1/3/6 bottles) competes on price.
**Solution:** Compete on **Value** (Protocol) and **Speed** (Upsells).

---

## 1. Core Offer Restructuring (The "Protocol" Frame)
*Stop selling "Serum". Start selling "The Dormancy Protocol".*

### Tier 1: The "Trial" (1 Month) - $59 (Price Hike)
*   **Old Price:** $49 + Ship.
*   **New Price:** $59 + Free Shipping.
*   **Psychology:** Higher price = Higher perceived efficacy. "Cheap" hair loss stuff feels fake.

### Tier 2: The "Regrowth Kit" (3 Months) - $149 ($49/mo)
*   **Bonus:** Free "Micro-Needling Scalp Roller" (Physical).
*   **Why:** The roller increases absorption by 300%. It makes the serum work better.
*   **Cost of Goods (COGS):** Roller costs ~$2. Perceived value: $25.

### Tier 3: The "Transformation Bundle" (6 Months) - $249 ($41/mo)
*   **Bonuses:**
    1.  Free Scalp Roller.
    2.  Free "Biotin & Collagen" Gummies (White label).
    3.  Free "Hair Hackers Bible" (Physical Book or Printed Insert).
*   **Guarantee:** 180-Day "Growth or Money Back" (Conditional on using 6 months).

## 2. Post-Purchase Upsell Flow (One-Click Upsells)
*The most profitable pixels on the internet.*

### OCU 1: The "Accelerator" (Hardware)
**Offer:** "Add the LED Red Light Growth Cap?"
**Pitch:** "You have the fuel (Serum). Now you need the engine (Light). Clinical studies show Red Light Therapy accelerates follicle stimulation by 40%."
**Price:** ~~$199~~ -> **$67** (One-time discount).
**Take Rate Goal:** 15%.
**COGS:** ~$15-20 (AliExpress/Dropship) or White Label.

### Downsell 1 (If OCU 1 Rejected):
**Offer:** "Okay, skip the cap. Get a double supply of Serum for 50% off?"
**Pitch:** "Stock up for the 'Shedding Phase' (Months 3-4)."
**Price:** $29/bottle (Bulk rate).

### OCU 2: The "Insurance" (Digital Info)
**Offer:** "The DHT Blocker Diet Protocol (Video Course)."
**Pitch:** "Topical treatment handles the surface. But are you poisoning your hair from the inside? Learn the 5 foods that spike DHT."
**Price:** $19.
**Margin:** 100%.
**Take Rate Goal:** 25%.
