# BusyBits Newsletter - Sponsorship System

## Newsletter Stats (Context)
- **Subscribers:** ~100,000
- **Open Rate Target:** 50% (Active cleaning required)
- **Niche:** Productivity x Health ("High Performance for Busy People")
- **Audience:** Professionals, Founders, Type-A Personalities.

## Strategy: Inbound-Ready Outbound
Stop waiting for sponsors. Pitch high-fit brands directly with a "Partner, not Advertiser" angle.

---

## 1. The "Perfect Sponsor" Avatar
Focus on brands that solve "Time" or "Energy" problems.
1.  **Health Tech:** Whoop, Oura, Eight Sleep, Levels.
2.  **Productivity SaaS:** Notion, Superhuman, Motion, Sunsama.
3.  **DTC Nutrition (Clean/Fast):** AG1, Huel, Magic Spoon, Ketone-IQ.
4.  **High-Ticket Wellness:** Saunas (Plunge), Red Light (Joovv).

---

## 2. Cold Outreach Scripts (The "Fan" Angle)
*Goal: Get a reply, not a sale instantly.*

### Script A: The "User" (Best for brands you actually use)
**Subject:** Who handles partnerships at [Brand]?

Hey [Brand Team],

I’ve been using [Product] for [Timeframe] and genuinely love it. (The [Specific Feature] saved me last week).

I write a newsletter called **BusyBits** (~100k subscribers, mostly high-performance professionals).

I’m planning my Q2 sponsorship calendar and would love to feature you guys. I think my audience would actually convert because they’re obsessed with [Benefit of Product].

Are you currently doing newsletter partnerships?

Best,
Ziga

### Script B: The "Competitor" (FOMO)
**Subject:** Idea for [Brand] x BusyBits

Hey [Name],

I saw you guys sponsoring [Competitor Newsletter/Podcast]. It caught my eye because my audience at **BusyBits** (100k subs) is almost identical, but we haven't featured a [Product Category] partner yet.

We typically see [Stat, e.g., 2-3% CTR] on relevant tech placements.

Would be open to a small test run to see if we can beat your current CPA?

Cheers,
Ziga

## 3. The "No-Brainer" Package (Rate Card Strategy)
*Don't just sell "ad slots". Sell "Campaigns".*

**Standard Slot:** $2,500
- 1x Primary Ad (Top of fold)
- Logo in "Trusted By" section

**The "Takeover" Package:** $9,000 (Discounted from $10k)
- 4x Consecutive Primary Ads (Builds frequency/trust)
- 1x "Deep Dive" Dedicated Email (or Section)
- 1x Social Shoutout (Twitter/LinkedIn)

**The "Performance" Kicker (For skeptics):**
- Base Fee: $1,000 (Covers cost)
- CPA Kicker: +$50 per conversion (Tracked via pixel/code)
- *Why:* Low risk for them, high upside for you if you deliver.
