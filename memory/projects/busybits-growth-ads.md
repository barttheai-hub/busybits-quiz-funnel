# BusyBits Newsletter - Paid Growth Strategy (Acquisition)

**Objective:** Acquire high-quality subscribers (LTV > CAC) to scale the list to 200k+.
**Target CPA:** <$2.00 per subscriber (Tier 1 Geos).
**Monetization:** Immediate liquidation via "SparkLoop" (Upscribe) or Self-Liquidating Offer (SLO) on the Thank You Page.

## The "Lead Magnet" Strategy (Lowering CPA)
People rarely subscribe just for "another newsletter". They subscribe for *utility*.
We need a **"High Performance OS"** asset to hook them.

**Asset:** "The CEO Morning Routine & Tech Stack" (PDF/Notion Template).
**Hook:** "Steal the exact protocols used by 50+ Unicorn Founders to optimize sleep, focus, and energy."
