# Hair Loss Funnel - VSL Visual Assets & Production Brief

**Objective:** Equip the video editor with a clear "Shot List" for the 9-minute Hair Loss VSL.
**Style:** High-End Docu-Series (Think: Alex Hormozi meets Vsauce).
**Tone:** Scientific, Serious, but Hopeful.

---

## Part 1: The Animations (Custom Assets)
*These need to be created in After Effects/Blender or sourced from stock.*

### Asset A: "The Kink in the Hose" (Metaphor)
-   **Description:** A hyper-realistic 3D render of a green garden hose lying on grass.
-   **Action:** Camera zooms in on a tight "kink". Water flow stops completely. Grass around the nozzle turns brown and withers.
-   **Resolution:** The hose unkinks. Water bursts out. Grass turns lush green instantly.
-   **Text Overlay:** "FLOW STOPPED = GROWTH STOPPED" -> "UNKINK = GROWTH".

### Asset B: "Scalp Tension Map" (Anatomy)
-   **Description:** A 3D model of a human head (transparent skin).
-   **Action:** The Galea Aponeurotica (top scalp muscle) glows RED and tightens, squeezing the blood vessels below.
-   **Outcome:** The hair follicles on top shrink and turn grey. The follicles on the side (where muscle is loose) stay green/healthy.
-   **Text Overlay:** "MICRO-INFLAMMATION" -> "OXYGEN STARVATION".

### Asset C: "The Peptide Mechanism" (Molecular)
-   **Description:** Inside the follicle (Macro shot).
-   **Action:** Blue glowing particles (Peptides) enter the shriveled root.
-   **Outcome:** The root expands, pulses with light, and pushes a thick black hair shaft upward through the skin.
-   **Text Overlay:** "ANAGEN SWITCH ACTIVATED".

## Part 2: Stock Footage (B-Roll)
*Source from Storyblocks/Artlist/Pexels.*

### Scene: The Problem (Agitation)
1.  **"Mirror Check":** Man (30s) looking in bathroom mirror, lifting hair to check recession. Lighting: Dim/Moody. Face: Worried.
2.  **"Pillow Shed":** Close up of white pillowcase with dark hairs on it. Hand brushes them off.
3.  **"Hat Reliance":** Man putting on a baseball cap before answering the door.
4.  **"Failed Treatments":** Montage of messy foam application (gross texture), pill bottles with "Warning" labels, man holding head in hands (headache/side effects).

### Scene: The Solution (Hope)
1.  **"Serum Application":** Clean, bright bathroom. Man applying dropper to scalp. Liquid is clear, watery (not greasy). Absorbs instantly.
2.  **"Scalp Massage":** Man using fingertips to massage scalp. Eyes closed, looking relaxed.
3.  **"Confidence":** Man walking down street, wind blowing in hair, smiling. No hat.
4.  **"Social Proof":** Quick cuts of 3 different men giving a "Thumbs Up" or showing hairline to camera.

## Part 5: The "Founder" A-Roll (Filming Guide)
*If Ziga films himself.*
-   **Setting:** Clean office or modern living room. Depth of field (blurred background).
-   **Lighting:** Key light 45 degrees. Hair light (rim light) to accentuate hair texture.
-   **Wardrobe:** Simple solid t-shirt (Black/Grey) or casual button-down.
-   **Performance:**
    -   Eye contact with lens.
    -   Use hand gestures.
    -   Vary pace (Slow down for serious points, speed up for excitement).
    -   **Prop:** Hold the bottle. Show the dropper. Demonstrate the "non-greasy" texture on hand.
