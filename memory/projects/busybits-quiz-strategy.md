# BusyBits Quiz Strategy: "The Performance Bottleneck"

**Goal:** Segment the newsletter list by psychographic "Type" (Time, Energy, or Focus) to send hyper-relevant content and increase open rates.
**Tool:** Typeform / Tally.
**Lead Magnet:** Personalized "Fix Your Bottleneck" Protocol.

---

## The Hook
**Headline:** Why aren't you hitting your goals yet?
**Subtext:** It's not willpower. It's a system bottleneck. Find yours in 60 seconds.

---

## The Questions (Logic)

### Q1: What's the first thing you feel when you wake up?
A. "Already behind schedule." (Time)
B. "Groggy and slow." (Energy)
C. "Distracted by 50 thoughts." (Focus)

### Q2: What kills your productivity the most?
A. Too many meetings. (Time)
B. 3 PM crash. (Energy)
C. Social media / Slack pings. (Focus)

### Q3: If you had a magic wand, what would you ask for?
A. A 30-hour day. (Time)
B. Endless stamina. (Energy)
C. Laser vision. (Focus)

---

## The Outcomes (Segmentation Tags)

### Type A: "The Time Starved Founder"
**Tag:** `segment:time`
**Problem:** High output, low leverage. Drowning in ops.
**Protocol:** "The Delegation & Automation Pack".
**Recommended Tool:** Notion / Zapier.
**Recommended Content:** Systems thinking, hiring SOPs.

### Type B: "The Energy Crasher"
**Tag:** `segment:energy`
**Problem:** Burnout cycles. High peaks, deep valleys.
**Protocol:** "The Circadian Reset".
**Recommended Tool:** Oura Ring / Eight Sleep.
**Recommended Content:** Biohacking, sleep optimization, nutrition.

### Type C: "The Monkey Mind"
**Tag:** `segment:focus`
**Problem:** Starts 10 things, finishes 0.
**Protocol:** "The Deep Work Environment".
**Recommended Tool:** Opal / Superhuman.
**Recommended Content:** Dopamine detox, flow state triggers.
