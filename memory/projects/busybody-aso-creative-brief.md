# BusyBody ASO Creative Brief (Visuals)

**Objective:** Create high-converting App Store screenshots that emphasize "Speed" and "Cleanliness."
**Style:** Minimalist, Dark Mode, High Contrast (White/Blue/Green on Black).
**Device Frame:** iPhone 16 Pro (Titanium Black).

---

## Slide 1: The "Hero" (Speed Hook)
**Goal:** Stop the scroll. Show the main value prop instantly.
**Headline:** Track Your Day in Seconds.
**Subtext:** The fastest calorie counter on the App Store.
**Visual:**
-   Main Dashboard view.
-   Highlight the "Calories Left" big number (e.g., 1,850 kcal).
-   Show Macro Rings (Protein/Carb/Fat) filled colorfully.
-   *Floating Element:* A notification bubble saying "Logged: Chicken Salad (3s)".

## Slide 2: The "Search" (Database Trust)
**Goal:** Prove we have the foods they eat.
**Headline:** Massive Food Database.
**Subtext:** Verified calories and macros for millions of items.
**Visual:**
-   Search screen with keyboard open.
-   Input: "Chic..."
-   Results List: Chicken Breast, Chicken Thigh, Chicken Salad, Chick-fil-A.
-   *Floating Element:* Green checkmarks next to verified items.

## Slide 3: The "Macro" (Niche Appeal)
**Goal:** Appeal to the fitness/gym crowd (Protein focus).
**Headline:** Hit Your Protein Goals.
**Subtext:** Visualize your nutrition at a glance.
**Visual:**
-   Detail view of the "Nutrition" tab.
-   Bar chart showing Protein/Carb/Fat breakdown.
-   Highlight the "Protein" bar being full (Green).
-   *Callout:* "180g Protein Hit" badge.

## Slide 4: The "Anti-Feature" (Differentiation)
**Goal:** Attack the competitors (MyFitnessPal) indirectly.
**Headline:** No Ads. No Bloat.
**Subtext:** We don't try to be a social network.
**Visual:**
-   Settings/Profile screen showing "Clean Mode".
-   A simple list of what we DON'T have:
    -   ❌ No Social Feed
    -   ❌ No Popups
    -   ❌ No Articles
-   *Visual Metaphor:* A very clean, empty white space vs a cluttered background (subtle).

## Slide 5: The "Progress" (Retention)
**Goal:** Show the long-term benefit.
**Headline:** Watch Yourself Transform.
**Subtext:** Track weight and consistency over time.
**Visual:**
-   Line graph showing weight going down over 3 months.
-   Calendar view with a "Streak" (Fire emoji) of 14 days.
-   *Callout:* "-12 lbs" badge.

---

**Color Palette:**
-   Background: #0f172a (Slate 950 - Dark Blue/Black)
-   Primary Accent: #3b82f6 (Blue 500 - Trust/Tech)
-   Success Green: #22c55e (Green 500 - Health/Go)
-   Text: #f8fafc (Slate 50 - White)

**Font:**
-   Inter / SF Pro Display (Bold, Tight Tracking).

**Mockup Style:**
-   Clay device mockups (Matte finish) or Realistic Titanium.
-   Slight 3D perspective rotation (Isometric) for dynamic feel.
