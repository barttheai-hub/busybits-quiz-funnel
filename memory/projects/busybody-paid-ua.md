# BusyBody App - Paid User Acquisition (UA) Strategy

**Objective:** Drive 10,000 active users in Month 1 to trigger App Store algorithm momentum.
**Target CPI (Cost Per Install):** <$2.50 (Blended).
**Target CPA (Activation - First Log):** <$5.00.

---

## Channel 1: Apple Search Ads (High Intent)
*Capture users specifically looking for a solution right now.*

### Campaign Structure
**1. Brand Defense (Exact Match)**
-   **Keywords:** "BusyBody", "Busy Body", "BusyBody Tracker".
-   **Bid:** High (Protect the name).

**2. Competitor Conquesting (The "Alternative" Play)**
-   **Keywords:** "MyFitnessPal", "LoseIt", "Cronometer", "FatSecret", "Yazio".
-   **Creative Set:** Custom Product Page (CPP) showing comparison charts ("Faster than X").
-   **Bid:** Aggressive ($3-5 CPT) to steal dissatisfied users.

**3. Generic Discovery (Volume)**
-   **Keywords:** "Calorie Counter", "Macro Tracker", "Food Log", "Diet App", "Keto Tracker".
-   **Bid:** Moderate ($2-3 CPT).

---

## Channel 2: Meta/TikTok (Visual Awareness)
*Disrupt the feed with the "Speed" value prop.*

### Ad Concept A: "The Speed Test" (Split Screen)
**Visual:**
-   **Left Screen (Competitor):** User opens app -> Wait for load -> Close popup ad -> Search "Chicken" -> Scroll past 5 branded options -> Click Log. (Timer: 45 seconds).
-   **Right Screen (BusyBody):** User opens app -> Search "Chicken" -> Log. (Timer: 4 seconds).
**Text Overlay:** "Stop wasting your life logging lunch."
**Caption:** "The fastest calorie counter ever built. No ads. No bloat. Just data."

### Ad Concept B: "The Minimalist Aesthetic" (Static)
**Visual:** A beautiful, clean screenshot of the Daily Dashboard on a sleek iPhone mockup. Dark mode enabled.
**Text:** "Zero Ads. Zero Social Feed. 100% Focus."
**Caption:** "Finally, a tracker that respects your time. Download Free."

### Ad Concept C: "The Founder's Apology" (UGC)
**Visual:** Ziga talking to camera.
**Script:**
"I'm sorry. I'm sorry that every other fitness app tries to sell you premium coaching or show you ads for insurance.
I built BusyBody to fix that. It does one thing: Tracks your macros. Fast.
If you hate bloat, this is for you."

---

## Channel 3: App Store Optimization (Conversion)
*Ensure paid traffic actually installs.*

**Icon:** Minimalist, high-contrast bolt or abstract food icon. (Stand out from the "Green Apple" clichés).
**Screenshots:**
-   Screen 1: "Log in Seconds" (Focus on Search UI).
-   Screen 2: "Visualize Your Macros" (Chart view).
-   Screen 3: "No Ads. Ever." (Trust signal).

---

## Measurement & Attribution
**Events to Track:**
1.  **Install** (Volume).
2.  **Sign Up** (Registration).
3.  **First Log** (Activation - The "Aha" Moment).
4.  **Day 7 Retention** (Quality).

**Budget Allocation (Launch Week):**
-   **ASA:** 40% (Capture intent).
-   **Meta:** 40% (Drive scale).
-   **TikTok:** 20% (Test viral potential).
