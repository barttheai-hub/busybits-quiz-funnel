# Hair Loss Funnel - "Stress-Less Scalp" Audio Script

## Format
**Type:** Guided Visualization / Somatic Release.
**Length:** ~10 Minutes.
**Tone:** Calm, Scientific, Reassuring. (Think: Huberman meets Meditation).

---

## 0:00 - Introduction (The Setup)
*(Soft ambient drone music fades in)*

"Welcome to the Stress-Less Scalp protocol.
Most people don't realize that they hold tension in their scalp muscles, just like they do in their shoulders.
This tension constricts blood flow to the follicles, choking them off from nutrients.
For the next 10 minutes, we are going to systematically release that tension, 'unkinking the hose' so your hair can breathe again.
Find a comfortable seat. Close your eyes. Let's begin."

---

## 1:30 - The Body Scan (Shoulders & Jaw)
"First, bring your attention to your shoulders. Drop them.
Often, when we are stressed, our shoulders rise up, creating a chain reaction of tightness up the neck.
Now, move to your jaw. Unclench your teeth. Let your tongue fall from the roof of your mouth.
The jaw muscle (masseter) connects directly to the fascia on the side of your head. Relaxing the jaw relaxes the scalp."

---

## 3:30 - The Scalp Release (Visualisation)
"Now, bring your focus to the very top of your head. The crown.
Imagine the skin there is tight, like a drum.
On your next exhale, imagine that skin softening. Becoming loose.
Picture the blood vessels underneath dilating, opening up.
Warm, nutrient-rich blood is flowing freely to every single follicle.
Feel the warmth spreading from your crown... down to your temples... down to the back of your neck."

---

## 6:00 - The "Micro-Movement" (Physical Action)
"Keeping your eyes closed, I want you to raise your eyebrows as high as you can. Hold for 3 seconds.
Now release. Feel the scalp slide back down.
Now, frown deep. Squeeze your eyes shut. Hold.
And release.
This physical contraction and release helps break up the 'galea aponeurotica'—the fibrous tissue that gets stuck down on the skull."

---

## 8:00 - Affirmation & Future Pacing
"You are creating the perfect environment for growth.
Your body knows how to grow hair. It just needed the signal that it's safe to do so.
You are safe. The tension is gone. The flow is restored."

---

## 9:30 - Outro
"When you're ready, take one deep breath in... and out.
Open your eyes.
Carry this looseness with you. If you feel stress today, remember: Drop the jaw. Soften the scalp.
Great work."
*(Music fades out)*
