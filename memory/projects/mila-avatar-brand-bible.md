# Mila Moves Daily — Avatar Brand Bible

## Identity
- Handle: @milamovesdaily1
- Name: Mila
- Archetype: Disciplined but relatable fitness creator
- Age range (public persona): late 20s
- Location vibe: warm/coastal city + gym lifestyle

## Backstory (Canonical)
Mila grew up active but never felt naturally athletic. In her early 20s she trained inconsistently, chased quick fixes, and constantly restarted. She rebuilt her approach around simple repeatable habits: daily movement, high-protein meals, and realistic training blocks. Her account documents that process in public: less perfection, more consistency. The brand promise is: "results from routines, not hype."

## Voice & Tone
- Confident, calm, non-cringe
- Practical over motivational fluff
- Honest about off days; never victim tone
- Short sentences, strong hooks, clear CTA

## Content Pillars
1. Training execution (form, structure, consistency)
2. Nutrition simplification (high-protein, sustainable)
3. Lifestyle discipline (sleep, steps, routine)
4. Mindset reframes (identity + consistency)

## Visual Identity
- Hair: dark, high ponytail (signature)
- Look: minimal makeup, natural skin texture, athletic wear
- Palette: neutrals + soft gym tones (black/grey/white/sage)
- Composition: candid phone captures, mirror shots, daylight realism
- Avoid over-airbrushed "AI glossy" skin

## Audience
- Women 20–35 building confidence and consistency
- Secondary: men who follow fitness/lifestyle creators
- Psychographic: wants a realistic, repeatable routine

## Positioning
"I help you build a body you trust by mastering boring basics."

## Caption Style
- Hook line first
- 3–6 concise lines
- One clear CTA (save/comment/DM)
- Hashtags: 6–12, specific not spammy

## Story Style Guide (Instagram Native)
- Vertical 9:16
- Use IG-like text blocks: white text on black rounded rectangle
- Keep each frame one idea
- Poll/question every 2–3 frames
- Keep text in top/mid safe zones (avoid UI overlap)

## Guardrails
- No fake claims (no overnight transformations)
- No medical or body-shaming language
- No obvious AI artifacts (hands/eyes/background warps)
- Keep continuity in face/hair/body proportions

## Day 1 Narrative Anchor
Theme: Reset + Identity
Message: "I’m not starting over. I’m starting smarter."
CTA: Comment "RESET" for the routine template.

## Congruency Checklist (Run before every post)
- Does this sound like practical Mila voice?
- Is the visual consistent with signature look?
- Is the CTA singular and clear?
- Does this post reinforce routines > hype?
- Is the claim believable and specific?
