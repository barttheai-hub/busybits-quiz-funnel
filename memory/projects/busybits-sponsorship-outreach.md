## Outreach Workflow (The "20 in 60" Protocol)

**Time Block:** 60 Minutes.
**Action:** Send 20 customized messages.

**Step 1: Find the Decision Maker (LinkedIn)**
-   Search: "[Brand Name] + Partnerships / Marketing Manager / Growth Lead"
-   Filter: "People" -> "Current Company".

**Step 2: The "Soft Ping" (LinkedIn DM / Twitter DM)**
*If open DMs:*
"Hey [Name], huge fan of [Brand]. I write BusyBits (100k subs). Looking for the right person to chat partnerships with—is that you or someone else on the team?"

**Step 3: The Email (If DM fails)**
*Tool: Hunter.io / Apollo to find email.*
**Subject:** Partnership Q / [Brand] x BusyBits?
"Hey [Name],
I'll keep this short.
I run **BusyBits**, a newsletter for 100k+ high-performance founders and execs.
I use [Brand] personally (it's awesome).
I think my audience would be a perfect fit for a Q2 campaign.
Are you the right person to chat with about testing a sponsorship?
Best,
Ziga"

**Step 4: Follow Up (3 Days later)**
"Bump on this. Just planning out the April calendar and wanted to give you first right of refusal before I reach out to [Competitor]. (I honestly prefer your product)."
