# BusyBits subject-line bank (next 5 sends)

Use these as A/B/C candidates. Keep preheaders short and curiosity-driven; avoid spammy words.

## Send 1 ("warm restart" / value-forward)
1) The 10-minute workout I’d do if I was starting over
2) Your next 7 days: simple plan, zero fluff
3) The mistake that kept me "stuck" for months

Preheader ideas:
- Screenshot this. Then do it tomorrow.
- Two habits. That’s it.

## Send 2 ("myth bust" / contrarian)
1) Most people diet like this (and it backfires)
2) The “healthy” habit that’s slowing your progress
3) This is why your steps aren’t working

Preheader ideas:
- Fix this one lever first.

## Send 3 ("identity" / social proof)
1) What fit people do *before* they feel motivated
2) If you can do this, you’ll stay consistent
3) The easiest way to become "a person who trains"

Preheader ideas:
- Motivation is optional.

## Send 4 ("mini challenge" / engagement)
1) 48-hour challenge: do this once
2) Try this tonight (takes 3 minutes)
3) Reply with a number (quick)

Preheader ideas:
- I’ll tell you what it means.

## Send 5 ("reactivation-friendly" / clean hook)
1) Still want me to send these?
2) Quick check-in (1 click)
3) I don’t want to spam you

Preheader ideas:
- Tell me what you want more of.
- One-click preference update.
