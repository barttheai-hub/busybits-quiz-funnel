# BusyBits cold-subscriber reactivation (3-email ladder)

Target: Cold segment (no opens in 180 days). Goal: improve deliverability + focus on engaged readers.

## Email 1 — “Still want this?” (one-click keep me)
**Subject options (pick 1):**
- Still want BusyBits?
- Should I stop emailing you?
- Quick check-in

**Body (copy/paste):**
Hey — quick check.

I only want to send BusyBits to people who actually want it.

If you still want to keep getting BusyBits, click here:
→ **Yes, keep sending BusyBits**

If not, no worries — you don’t need to do anything and I’ll stop emailing you.

— Ziga

CTA link should tag the subscriber as “Keep” (or move back to Warm/Engaged).

---

## Email 2 — “Best of BusyBits” (pure value)
**Subject options:**
- The 5 best BusyBits you missed
- Best of BusyBits (quick hits)
- In case you missed these

**Body:**
If you haven’t been reading BusyBits lately, here are 5 of the best things we’ve sent recently:

1) [LINK] — (1 sentence why it’s useful)
2) [LINK] — (1 sentence)
3) [LINK] — (1 sentence)
4) [LINK] — (1 sentence)
5) [LINK] — (1 sentence)

Want to keep getting BusyBits?
→ **Yes, keep sending**

---

## Email 3 — “I’ll remove you tomorrow” (final)
**Subject options:**
- Last email
- I’ll remove you tomorrow
- Do you still want this?

**Body:**
Last note from me.

Because you haven’t opened BusyBits in a while, I’m going to remove you tomorrow to keep the list clean.

If you want to stay, click here:
→ **Keep me subscribed**

If you don’t click, you’ll be unsubscribed.

— Ziga

## Implementation notes
- Send to Cold 180d+ only.
- Suppress anyone who clicks “keep me”.
- After Email 3: automatically unsubscribe remaining.
- Track: % kept, spam complaints, deliverability/open rate lift on core list.
