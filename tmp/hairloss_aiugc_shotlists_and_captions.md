# Hair-loss AI UGC — shotlists + caption overlays (for 3 scripts)

General style:
- Handheld, slight shake, natural light, imperfect audio
- Cuts every 1–2s
- Captions always on; highlight key words

---

## Script 1 — “I didn’t want to take finasteride…” (35–45s)

### Shotlist (scene-by-scene)
0–2s: Selfie close-up, quick head tilt showing temples
- On-screen: “Hairline moving?”

2–6s: Cut to bathroom mirror angle, messy counter
- Say: “I didn’t want to take finasteride… here’s what I wish I knew.”

6–10s: Cut to old photo (phone screen) or “scrolling” motion
- Say: “I tried random shampoos and supplements for months.”

10–18s: Talking head, slightly off-center
- Say: “What helped was understanding *why* it happens and picking a plan.”

18–26s: B-roll: writing checklist on notepad / sticky note
- On-screen checklist bullets appear:
  - “Stop the driver”
  - “Support regrowth”
  - “Track progress”

26–34s: B-roll: taking a photo in the same spot (tripod or taped phone)
- Say: “Weekly photos in the same lighting—game changer.”

34–45s: Talking head + CTA
- Say: “Want the exact checklist so you don’t waste months guessing? Tap below.”

### Caption overlays (quick)
- “I didn’t want to take finasteride…”
- “I wasted months guessing”
- “Pick ONE plan + track weekly”
- “Get the checklist ↓”

## Script 2 — “3 mistakes I made” (30–40s)

### Shotlist
0–2s: Selfie, quick zoom-in
- On-screen: “3 hair-loss mistakes”

2–6s: Talking head
- Say: “I wasted 6 months doing the wrong thing.”

6–16s: Rapid cuts (each mistake gets 2–3s)
- Mistake 1: holding 2 different products
- Mistake 2: scrolling camera roll (no consistent photos)
- Mistake 3: calendar with random gaps

16–28s: Talking head
- Say: “Now I do the boring stuff that works: one plan, long enough, measure weekly.”

28–40s: CTA
- Say: “Want my ‘do this first’ checklist? Tap below.”

### Caption overlays
- “Mistake #1: switching too fast”
- “Mistake #2: no tracking”
- “Mistake #3: inconsistency”
- “One plan. Enough time.”

---

## Script 3 — “Do this first checklist” (40–50s)

### Shotlist
0–2s: POV shot placing phone on shelf
- On-screen: “Do this FIRST”

2–8s: Talking head
- Say: “If your hairline is moving back, do this first.”

8–22s: Step-by-step b-roll (each step 3–4s)
- Step 1: taking baseline photos (front/top/temples)
- Step 2: writing “ONE plan” on note
- Step 3: setting weekly reminder

22–35s: Talking head
- Say: “Track weekly, not daily. Missing days kills progress.”

35–50s: CTA
- Say: “I put this into a one-page checklist. Tap below and I’ll send it.”

### Caption overlays
- “Baseline photos (same lighting)”
- “Choose ONE plan”
- “Track weekly”
- “Consistency > intensity”
- “Get the 1-page checklist ↓”

---

## Testing pack (9 ads)
Use the 3 angles x 3 hooks matrix:
- Hooks: #2 (do this first) / #7 (skeptical about fin) / #10 (weekly photos)
- Angles: skeptical / mistakes / routine

Keep everything else identical per batch (same caption style, same CTA) to isolate what’s working.
