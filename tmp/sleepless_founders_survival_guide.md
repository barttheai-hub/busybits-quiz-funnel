# The Sleepless Founder's Survival Guide
## How to Operate at 90% Capacity on 4 Hours of Sleep
*By Dr. Ziga (MD)*

---

**⚠️ DISCLAIMER:**
This is not medical advice. This is a **survival protocol**. Do not do this every day. Chronic sleep deprivation kills performance (and you). Use this only when shipping, launching, or putting out fires.

---

### 1. The Morning Protocol (Do Not Hit Snooze)
The moment your eyes open, your brain is flooded with adenosine (sleep pressure). You need to clear it, not mask it.

*   **Step 1: The Cold Shock (0-5 mins)**
    *   Do not look at your phone.
    *   Get out of bed immediately.
    *   Splash ice-cold water on your face or take a 60-second cold shower. This spikes adrenaline and cortisol to wake you up.

*   **Step 2: The Light Blast (5-15 mins)**
    *   Get sunlight in your eyes immediately. 10-15 minutes.
    *   No sun? Use a 10,000 lux sad lamp.
    *   *Why:* Sets your circadian clock so you can actually sleep tonight.

*   **Step 3: Hydrate First, Caffeinate Later (15-30 mins)**
    *   Drink 500ml of water with electrolytes (sodium/potassium).
    *   **CRITICAL:** Wait 90 minutes before coffee.
    *   *Why:* If you drink coffee immediately, you block adenosine receptors but don't clear the chemical. When the caffeine wears off at 2pm, you will crash harder. Let your body clear it naturally first.

### 2. The "Napuccino" (The Secret Weapon)
Use this between 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM when the crash hits.

1.  Drink a double espresso rapidly.
2.  Immediately set a timer for 20 minutes.
3.  Lie down and close your eyes (even if you don't sleep).
4.  **Wake up exactly when the alarm rings.**

*The Science:* The nap clears adenosine. The caffeine takes 20 minutes to hit your bloodstream. You wake up with a cleared brain *and* a caffeine kick.

### 3. The Supplement Stack (Focus & Jitters)
Caffeine makes you wired but anxious. You need focus.

*   **L-Theanine (200mg):** Take this with your coffee. It smooths out the jitters and promotes alpha brain waves (flow state). Ratio: 2:1 Theanine to Caffeine.
*   **L-Tyrosine (500mg-1g):** Dopamine precursor. Helps with working memory under stress.
*   **Creatine (5g):** Not just for muscles. Sleep-deprived brains run low on energy (ATP). Creatine restores it.

### 4. Nutrition: Avoid the Crash
Your hunger hormones (ghrelin) are spiked. You will crave carbs and sugar.

*   **The Rule:** **Zero carbs for breakfast/lunch.**
*   Eat high protein and healthy fats (eggs, avocado, steak).
*   Carbs = Insulin spike = Crash. Save carbs for dinner to help you sleep.

### 5. The Shutdown (Protect Tomorrow)
You survived today. Now ensure you don't ruin tomorrow.

*   **Caffeine Cutoff:** 10 hours before bed (absolute hard stop).
*   **Magnesium Glycinate (400mg):** Take 1 hour before bed to relax the nervous system.
*   **Phone Away:** Blue light will destroy what little melatonin you have.
