## Script 1 — “I was skeptical about fin/min” (35–45s)
**Hook:** “I didn’t want to take finasteride… here’s what I wish I knew.”

**Story (3–12s):**
I noticed my hairline creeping back and I kept telling myself it was stress.
Then I did the worst thing: I tried random shampoos and supplements for months.

**Mechanism (12–25s):**
What actually helped was understanding *why* hair loss happens and picking a plan:
- stop the hormone driver (for some people that’s finasteride)
- support regrowth (often minoxidil)
- and track progress the right way

**Proof (25–35s):**
I started taking weekly photos in the same lighting and that’s when I finally saw whether it was working.

**CTA (35–45s):**
If you want, I’ll share the exact checklist I used so you don’t waste months guessing.
Tap below.

Compliance notes:
- Avoid “regrow guaranteed” claims.
- Use “for some people / talk to a doctor” if needed.

## Script 2 — “3 mistakes” (30–40s)
**Hook:** “I wasted 6 months doing the wrong thing for hair loss.”

**Body:**
If you’re thinning, here are 3 mistakes I made:

1) I kept switching products every 2 weeks.
2) I didn’t track properly — no consistent photos.
3) I ignored the basics: consistency + enough time.

What I do now is boring but effective:
- pick one evidence-based plan
- run it for long enough
- measure weekly the same way

**CTA:**
Want my full ‘do this first’ checklist? Tap below.

---

## Script 3 — “The checklist / routine walkthrough” (40–50s)
**Hook:** “If your hairline is moving back, do this first.”

**Routine:**
Step 1: take baseline photos (front/top/temples) in the same lighting.
Step 2: pick ONE plan and commit (don’t stack 10 things).
Step 3: track weekly, not daily.
Step 4: if you’re using treatments, be consistent — missing days kills results.

**CTA:**
I put this into a one-page checklist you can follow.
Tap below and I’ll send it.

---

## Testing matrix (fast)
Test 3 variables only:
- Hook: #2 vs #7 vs #10
- Angle: skeptical vs mistakes vs routine
- CTA: “get checklist” vs “see my plan”

Run 9 ads (3 hooks x 3 angles) before over-optimizing.
