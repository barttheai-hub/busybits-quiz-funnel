# Hair Loss Funnel - Compliance & Claims Checklist (Meta/TikTok/FDA)

**Status:** APPROVED (2026-02-18)
**Goal:** Ensure ads and landing pages don't get banned while maximizing conversion.

## 1. The "Red Flag" Words (DO NOT USE)
*These trigger automatic disapproval or account bans.*
-   ❌ "Cure" (Baldness is a medical condition).
-   ❌ "Permanent" (Implies impossible results).
-   ❌ "Guaranteed Growth" (Implies 100% success rate).
-   ❌ "FDA Approved" (Unless it's a drug like Minoxidil - we are a cosmetic/peptide).
-   ❌ "Reverses Baldness" (Too strong).

## 2. The "Safe" Alternatives (USE THESE)
*Psychologically strong but compliant.*
-   ✅ "Awakens Dormant Follicles" (Action-oriented).
-   ✅ "Supports Thicker Looking Hair" (Cosmetic claim).
-   ✅ "Nourishes the Scalp Environment" (Mechanism).
-   ✅ "Combats the Appearance of Thinning" (Visual claim).
-   ✅ "90-Day Money Back Guarantee" (Financial claim - totally fine).

## 3. Image & Video Rules (Meta Policy)
-   **Before/Afters:**
    -   ❌ Zoomed in on body parts (Gross/Shock value).
    -   ❌ Unrealistic timelines ("Grew back in 3 days").
    -   ✅ Side-by-side lifestyle shots (Smiling man with hat vs without).
    -   ✅ User Generated Content (Selfie style) is safer than clinical photos.
-   **Zoom:** Do not zoom in on the scalp skin too close (triggers "Personal Health" flags).

## 4. Landing Page Disclaimers (Footer)
*Must be visible on every page.*
-   "Results may vary. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."
-   "The story depicted on this site and the person depicted in the story are not actual news. Rather, this story is based on the results that some people who have used these products have achieved."
-   "Testimonials found on this site are unverified results that have been forwarded to us by users..."

## 5. TikTok Specifics (Creative Center)
-   **No "Negative Self-Image":** Do not shame baldness. Focus on "Confidence" and "Upgrade".
-   **Music:** Use commercial library sounds only.
-   **Captions:** Keep them centered (Safe Zone).

## 6. Audit Checklist (Before Launch)
-   [ ] Are all claims substantiated by ingredients? (Peptides support density).
-   [ ] Is the Refund Policy clear? (90 Days).
-   [ ] Are terms like "Cure" removed?
-   [ ] Is the Privacy Policy link working?
