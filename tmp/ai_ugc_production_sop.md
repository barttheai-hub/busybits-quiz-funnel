# AI UGC Production SOP (Meta-native)

Goal: reliably generate Meta-native UGC ads (hooky, simple, believable) with fast iteration.

## 1) Inputs (brief)
For each offer, write:
- ICP: who + main pain
- Promise: desired outcome (time-bound if possible)
- Mechanism: why it works (1 sentence)
- Proof: 1–3 claims you can support
- CTA: what to do now

## 2) Creative angles (choose 3–5)
- “I was skeptical but…”
- “What I’d do if I was starting over”
- “3 mistakes I made”
- “Doctor / expert explains” (only if legit)
- “Before/after story” (only if compliant)
- “Routine walkthrough”

## 3) Script template (30–45s)
1) Hook (0–3s): pattern interrupt + pain
2) Relatable story (3–12s): who I am + what happened
3) Mechanism (12–25s): what changed + why it worked
4) Proof (25–35s): 1 data point / testimonial / observable result
5) CTA (35–45s): what to click + what you get

## 4) Prompting checklist (for video gen or actor)
- Language: casual, imperfect, spoken
- Visuals: handheld, natural light, messy background
- Wardrobe: normal (not “ad-y”)
- Audio: slight room tone (not too clean)
- Cut pace: 1–2s cuts, captions on

## 5) Variations to generate (minimum set)
For each concept:
- 3 hooks
- 2 mechanisms
- 2 CTAs
=> 12 variants quickly.

## 6) Post-production
- Captions: bold, 3–6 words per line
- On-screen text: mirror hook + key proof
- Add native UI elements (comments, likes) only if allowed

## 7) Testing plan (Meta)
- Broad + 1 interest stack
- Optimize for purchase/lead
- Kill rules: after X spend without CPI/CAC threshold
- Keep winners and iterate only one variable at a time

## 8) Compliance guardrails
- Avoid medical claims you can’t substantiate
- Avoid misleading before/after
- Avoid “guaranteed” outcomes
