## Send #2 — The hidden diet mistake
**Subject A:** Most people diet like this (and it backfires)
**Subject B:** The “healthy” habit slowing your progress
**Subject C:** If fat loss feels hard, check this

**Preheader:** One lever fixes a lot.

**Body:**
A common reason fat loss stalls isn’t willpower.
It’s this:

- People try to change everything at once
- Then they get hungry, tired, and inconsistent

Instead, pick ONE lever for 7 days:

- Protein at every meal (easy default)
- A fixed “last bite time” (e.g., 8pm)
- 2x 10‑minute walks per day

Action step: choose one lever and hit it for 7 days. No hero mode.
