# Hair-Loss Funnel: Bonus Content Assets (Offer Stack)

**Goal:** Increase perceived value and conversion rate by adding low-cost, high-value digital bonuses.

## Bonus 1: The "Doctor Prep" Cheat Sheet
*Format: Single-page PDF*
*Value Prop:* "Don't know what to ask your dermatologist? Use this checklist to get the best prescription plan for your specific hair type."

### Content Draft

**Headline:** The 5-Minute Dermatologist Prep Sheet
**Subhead:** Exactly what to ask to get the right treatment plan faster.

**Section 1: Your History (Check all that apply)**
- [ ] Maternal grandfather had hair loss
- [ ] Paternal grandfather had hair loss
- [ ] Noticed thinning for > 6 months
- [ ] Scalp feels itchy or inflamed
- [ ] Hair sheds when washing/brushing

**Section 2: The "Golden Questions" to Ask**
1. "Based on my pattern (temples vs crown), would I respond better to oral or topical treatments?"
2. "Are there any vitamin deficiencies (like Vitamin D or Iron) we should test for that might be accelerating this?"
3. "Can I combine microneedling with this prescription safely?"
4. "What is the earliest realistic timeline I should expect to see a pause in shedding?"

**Section 3: Red Flags to Mention**
- Mention if you have: Low libido, brain fog, or history of depression (important for Finasteride discussions).
- Mention if you have: Heart palpitations or low blood pressure (important for Minoxidil discussions).
